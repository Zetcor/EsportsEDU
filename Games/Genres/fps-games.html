Popular FPS Esports Games

Valorant
Riot Games | June 2, 2020 | Microsoft WindowsPlayStation 5
Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter developed by Riot Games that combines precise gunplay with unique agent abilities. Set in a near-future world, players choose from a diverse cast of agents, each with distinct skills that support different playstyles. The game features 5v5 matches where teams alternate between attacking and defending, aiming to plant or defuse a spike. Strategy, communication, and sharp aim are essential to outsmart and outplay opponents in high-stakes rounds.
Game Mechanics:
Valorant is a 5v5 tactical first-person shooter (FPS) where players choose unique characters called Agents, each with special abilities. The game blends precise gunplay with hero-based abilities. Gameplay revolves around teams alternating between attacking and defending roles. The attacking team’s objective is to plant a bomb called the "spike," while the defending team aims to prevent the plant or defuse the spike if it is planted. Matches are played in a best-of-25 rounds format, with the first team to win 13 rounds emerging victorious.
Featured Characters:

Sova

Initiator

Sova is a relentless hunter from Russia who uses advanced technology to track, scout, and eliminate enemies. With his bow and recon tools, he believes in precision and discipline, always ensuring his targets are found and neutralized. He values information and is calm under pressure, acting as the eyes of his team.

Jett

Duelist

Jett is a nimble and agile agent hailing from South Korea. Known for her cocky and quick-witted personality, she thrives on outmaneuvering opponents and taking high-risk plays. Her background includes working in a kitchen before an incident with her wind manipulation abilities revealed her powers to the world. Now, she fights with sharp precision and unmatched speed.

Game Tournament:
The Valorant Champions Tour (VCT) is the official competitive circuit for Valorant, created by Riot Games, which began in 2021 and follows a year-long structure culminating in a world championship.
Counter Strike 2
Valve Corporation | September 27, 2023 | Microsoft WindowsLinux
Counter-Strike 2 is the evolution of the iconic tactical shooter series, built on Valve’s Source 2 engine. It features fast-paced, high-stakes 5v5 gameplay where teams alternate between attacking and defending bomb sites. Known for its precise gunplay, minimal abilities, and strong emphasis on teamwork and strategy, CS2 maintains the core of what made CS:GO a competitive favorite while enhancing visuals and performance.
Game Mechanics:
Counter-Strike 2 is a 5v5 tactical first-person shooter that emphasizes precise gunplay, economy management, and team-based strategy. In each match, two teams—Terrorists (T) and Counter-Terrorists (CT)—compete with opposing objectives: the Terrorists aim to plant a bomb, while the Counter-Terrorists must defend the site or defuse the bomb. The first team to win 13 rounds in competitive mode claims victory. Core features include realistic weapons and recoil mechanics, while new additions in CS2 include the Source 2 engine, which enhances graphics, lighting, and smoke physics, as well as improved matchmaking, a higher tick rate, and an updated user interface and sound system.
Featured Characters:

Terrorists

Attacker

The main role of the Terrorists is to plant the bomb at a designated bombsite and defend it until it explodes. Alternatively, they can win the round by eliminating all Counter-Terrorists before the timer runs out. Strategy, coordination, and aggressive map control are key elements of their gameplay.

Counter-Terrorists

Defender

Counter-Terrorists aim to prevent the Terrorists from planting the bomb or to defuse it if planted. They can also win by eliminating all Terrorists or by letting the round timer expire without a bomb plant. CT gameplay emphasizes defense, positioning, and quick rotations between bomb sites.

Game Tournaments:
The Counter-Strike 2 Major Championship is the premier tournament series in CS2 esports, officially sponsored by Valve. These Majors are the most prestigious events in the Counter-Strike competitive scene, featuring the world's top teams competing for significant prize pools and global recognition.
Overwatch
Blizzard Entertainment | May 24, 2016| Microsoft WindowsPlayStation 5Nintendo SwitchPlaystation 4Xbox One
Overwatch is a team-based hero shooter by Blizzard where players choose from a diverse cast of heroes, each with unique abilities and roles. Teams of five compete in objective-based game modes, relying on coordination and hero synergy to win. With a colorful art style and fast-paced action, Overwatch blends FPS mechanics with MOBA-like team dynamics.
Game Mechanics:
Overwatch is a team-based hero shooter that originally featured 6v6 matches in Overwatch 1 and now uses a 5v5 format in Overwatch 2. The game’s objectives vary depending on the mode, including escorting payloads, capturing control points, or playing on hybrid maps that combine both elements. It uses a role-based system where players choose from three roles: Tank, who absorb damage and create space for the team; Damage, who focus on eliminating threats and dealing damage; and Support, who heal and provide buffs to keep teammates alive and effective in combat.
Featured Heroes:

Reinhardt

Tank

Reinhardt is a frontline tank hero clad in heavy armor and wielding a massive rocket hammer. He leads the charge with a powerful barrier shield that protects teammates from incoming fire. His playstyle revolves around close-quarters combat, area control, and initiating team fights. With strong presence and crowd control, Reinhardt is a cornerstone of many defensive and brawling team comps.

Tracer

Damage

Tracer is a lightning-fast damage hero who manipulates time to outmaneuver her enemies. She uses rapid blink dashes and a recall ability to zip around the battlefield and escape danger. Armed with dual pulse pistols, she excels at harassing backlines and picking off squishy targets. Tracer thrives in chaotic fights where her speed and unpredictability shine.

Game Tournaments:
The Overwatch League (OWL), launched in 2018, was one of the first city-based franchised esports leagues, modeled after traditional sports leagues, with teams representing cities such as New York, London, Shanghai, Seoul, and Los Angeles.
