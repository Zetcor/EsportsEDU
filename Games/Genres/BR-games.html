The game set the stage for the rise of the battle royale genre, where 100 players are dropped onto an island and fight to be the last one standing. The objective is to scavenge for weapons, armor, and other resources while staying within a shrinking safe zone. Players must eliminate others while avoiding being killed themselves, creating intense, strategic, and action-packed gameplay. PUBG is known for its realistic graphics, large-scale maps, and a variety of game modes, offering both solo and team-based experiences.
Game Mechanics:
In PUBG, players are dropped onto a large island without any equipment and must find weapons, ammunition, armor, and other items scattered throughout the map. As the game progresses, the playable area shrinks, forcing players into smaller zones and increasing the intensity of confrontations. The game can be played in several modes, including solo, duo, and squad, with players either working alone or as part of a team to survive. The map is filled with various environments such as towns, forests, and open fields, all of which present different tactical advantages and challenges. Players need to carefully manage their resources, including health items, and plan their movements to avoid being caught in the shrinking "blue zone." Combat is a mix of close-range, mid-range, and long-range encounters, requiring players to adapt their strategies based on available gear and environmental factors. Players can also drive vehicles, adding an extra layer of strategy to the gameplay. The last player or team remaining alive is crowned the winner.

Fortnite Battle Royale
Epic Games | September 26, 2017 | Mobile | Console | PC
Fortnite Battle Royale is a free-to-play online multiplayer battle royale game developed by Epic Games and released in 2017. In Fortnite, 100 players are dropped onto an island where they must fight to be the last one standing. Players can play solo, in duos, or in squads, working alone or with friends to gather resources, build structures, and eliminate opponents. The game features a unique combination of shooting mechanics and construction, allowing players to build walls, ramps, and other structures for defense and strategic advantage. Fortnite is known for its vibrant and cartoony art style, frequent updates, seasonal events, and a diverse selection of skins and cosmetics.
Game Mechanics:
In Fortnite, players are dropped onto a large map and must scavenge for weapons, materials, and items scattered throughout the environment. Players can mine resources (wood, stone, and metal) to build structures that provide protection, elevation, or tactical advantages. The playable area gradually shrinks, forcing players into close proximity with one another, leading to intense battles. The last player (or team) remaining is declared the winner. Fortnite features various weapons, from assault rifles and shotguns to sniper rifles and explosives, each with its own advantages and range. Players can also use vehicles (such as the Battle Bus or cars in certain modes) to traverse the map quickly. Additionally, the game introduces new items and mechanics regularly, keeping the gameplay fresh. The combination of shooting, building, and frequent updates makes Fortnite a fast-paced, dynamic experience.

Apex Legends
Respawn Entertainment | February 4, 2019 | Mobile | Console | PC
Apex Legends is a free-to-play battle royale game developed by Respawn Entertainment and released in 2019. Set in the futuristic world of the Titanfall universe, Apex Legends differentiates itself from other battle royales with its emphasis on team-based gameplay, unique character abilities, and high mobility. The game features squads of three players who select from a roster of "Legends," each with their own distinct abilities that shape the way players engage with the game. Players drop into a large map, scavenge for weapons, armor, and items, and battle to be the last squad standing. Apex Legends is known for its fast-paced combat, smooth movement mechanics, and tactical depth.
Game Mechanics:
In Apex Legends, 60 players are divided into 20 squads of 3 players each. Players are tasked with fighting their way across an ever-shrinking map, scavenging for weapons, armor, and other resources while eliminating rival squads. One of the key mechanics in Apex Legends is the use of Legends, each with unique abilities that provide strategic advantages, such as healing teammates, creating decoys, or deploying smoke screens. The game also introduces unique movement options, including wall-running, sliding, and using ziplines, which contribute to its dynamic and high-speed gameplay. The map features a variety of terrains, including open fields, dense forests, and urban environments, providing both cover and open spaces for combat. Players must carefully choose their landing spot to gather loot, plan their strategies, and position themselves advantageously. The shrinking "ring" forces players into more concentrated areas, ensuring that the game builds toward intense, final confrontations.
