Popular Mobile Esports Games

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang
Moonton | July 14, 2016 | Mobile
Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game designed for mobile devices. Players control a single character with unique abilities and battle against a team of opponents to destroy their base while protecting their own. The game features various modes including ranked matches, classic matches, and special seasonal game modes.
Game Mechanics:
In Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, players control heroes with unique abilities and roles in a 5v5 team battle format. The game mechanics focus on strategic teamwork, with players choosing from various heroes, each with specific strengths and weaknesses. Players earn gold and experience through kills, minion waves, and objectives, which are used to buy items and level up during the match. The goal is to destroy the enemy’s base while defending your own, with map control, positioning, and coordination being key to success.
Featured Heroes:

Hayabusa

Assassin

A swift and deadly ninja who excels at eliminating enemies with his shadow-based skills and quick escapes.

Cecilion

Mage

A powerful vampire mage who scales with mana, unleashing high burst damage from a distance with his bat-like magic.

Game Tournaments:
Mobile Legends has a well-structured competitive scene, led by the Mobile Legends Professional League (MPL), held in regions like the Philippines, Indonesia, and Malaysia. Aspiring teams and players compete in the MLBB Development League (MDL), which serves as a training ground for future MPL stars. Major tournaments like the Southeast Asia Cup (MSC) and the global M-Series World Championship (M1, M2, M3, etc.) showcase top-tier international competition and crown the best teams in the world.
Honor of Kings
TiMi Studio Group (Tencent Games) | November 26, 2015 | Mobile
Honor of Kings is one of the most popular and highest-grossing mobile games globally, especially dominant in China. Players pick from a large roster of heroes based on historical and mythological characters to compete in 5v5 matches. Developed by TiMi Studio Group (a subsidiary of Tencent), it offers fast-paced gameplay, rich lore, and strong competitive esports tournaments. An international version called "Honor of Kings: World" is also expanding globally.
Game Mechanics:
Honor of Kings features fast-paced 5v5 real-time battles where two teams compete to destroy each other’s base while defending their own. Before each match, players choose from a wide roster of heroes, each with unique abilities, roles, and playstyles that contribute to team strategy. The gameplay revolves around completing tactical objectives, such as pushing lanes, defeating jungle monsters, and taking down enemy towers and the core base. Competitive elements like ranked matchmaking and seasonal rewards keep players engaged and striving for progression each season.
Featured Heroes:

Li Bai

Assassin

A graceful and elusive assassin who uses fluid swordplay and mobility to quickly eliminate high-priority targets.

Gao

Mage

A powerful mage who manipulates arcane energy to deal burst magic damage and control the battlefield from a distance.

Arthur

Fighter

A durable frontline warrior wielding a holy sword, excelling in sustained combat and initiating fights for his team.

Game Tournaments:
Honor of Kings has a strong competitive scene led by the King Pro League (KPL), China’s top professional league featuring elite teams. On the international stage, tournaments like the Honor of Kings World Champion Cup (KCC) and the Honor of Kings International Championship (KIC) bring together top players from around the globe to compete for world titles. For aspiring professionals, the King Growth League (KGL) serves as a development league to nurture new talent and prepare them for higher-level play.
PUBG Mobile
PUBG Corporation | Krafton, Tencent, VNG Games | March 19, 2018 | Mobile
PUBG Mobile is a mobile adaptation of PlayerUnknown’s Battlegrounds (PUBG), a battle royale where 100 players parachute onto an island and fight to be the last one standing. Players scavenge for weapons, vehicles, and equipment while the safe zone shrinks over time. PUBG Mobile offers multiple maps, game modes, and frequent collaborations. It is a major player in mobile esports, with global tournaments like the PMGC (PUBG Mobile Global Championship).
Game Mechanics:
PUBG Mobile is a tactical battle royale shooter where up to 100 players are dropped onto a large map to fight for survival. Players scavenge for weapons, armor, and supplies while navigating a shrinking safe zone that forces encounters and strategic positioning. The game supports solo, duo, or squad-based play and emphasizes tactical movement, aim accuracy, and map awareness. Competitive elements like ranked matchmaking, seasonal rewards, and a variety of game modes keep the gameplay fresh and engaging.
Featured Heroes:

Victor

A special forces operative who reloads SMGs faster in EvoGround mode, making him ideal for close-quarters combat.

Sara

A skilled vehicle engineer whose ability reduces damage taken by vehicles she drives, giving her team better mobility and survivability.

Andy

A former magician turned gunslinger with enhanced weapon draw speed in specific game modes, allowing quicker combat engagement.

Game Tournaments:
PUBG Mobile has a competitive scene with regional leagues like the PUBG Mobile Pro League (PMPL) and global tournaments such as the PUBG Mobile Global Championship (PMGC). The PUBG Mobile World Invitational (PMWI) is an exclusive invitational event featuring top teams from around the world. For newcomers, the PUBG Mobile Club Open (PMCO) provides an open platform for amateurs and rising teams to compete and make a name for themselves.
