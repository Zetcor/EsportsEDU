Popular Fighting Esports Games

Tekken 8
Bandai Namco Studios | January 26, 2024 | Microsoft WindowsPlayStation 5Xbox
Tekken 8 is the latest installment in the long-running Tekken franchise, known for its 3D movement, detailed character models, and rich storylines. It continues the long-running story of the Mishima family—particularly the clash between Kazuya Mishima and his son Jin Kazama. This installment shifts toward aggressive gameplay, with the new Heat System giving characters temporary power-ups for more intense combat.
Game Mechanics:
Tekken 8 introduces the Heat System, a new mechanic that rewards aggression by giving characters enhanced abilities and chip damage during a limited power-up state. Players can enter Heat via a dedicated button or through certain attacks, enabling powerful follow-ups. The game also features recoverable health, which can be regained through offense, promoting nonstop action. Combined with streamlined movement and dynamic stages, Tekken 8 emphasizes fast-paced, offensive gameplay more than ever before.
Featured Characters:

Jin

Fighter

Jin is a central character in the Tekken series, known for his serious demeanor and tragic past. He is the son of Kazuya Mishima and Jun Kazama, inheriting both the cursed Devil Gene and a strong sense of justice. Jin’s fighting style blends traditional Karate with power and precision, reflecting his inner conflict between darkness and control. Throughout the series, he battles against his own bloodline and destiny, often standing as both a hero and an anti-hero.

Lili

Fighter

Lili, whose full name is Emilie De Rochefort, is the elegant yet fierce daughter of a wealthy oil magnate from Monaco. She uses a graceful acrobatic fighting style that combines street fighting with ballet-like movements. Despite her refined upbringing, Lili has a rebellious streak and seeks excitement through fighting, often clashing with her rival Asuka Kazama. Her flashy style and confident attitude make her a unique and popular character in the Tekken roster.

Game Tournament:
One of the premier tournaments featuring Tekken 8 is Evo Japan 2024, held from April 27 to 29 at Ariake GYM-EX in Tokyo. This event marked Tekken 8's debut on the Evo Japan stage, attracting top players from around the world.
Street Fighter 6
Capcom | June 2, 2023 | Microsoft WindowsPlayStation 5Xbox
The sixth main entry in the iconic Street Fighter franchise, Street Fighter 6 reinvents the formula with a modern art style, it introduces three core modes: World Tour (single-player story/exploration), Battle Hub (online matches/social), and Fighting Ground (arcade, versus, and training). It’s praised for accessibility (Modern control scheme for beginners) and deep strategy with its Drive System.
Game Mechanics:
Street Fighter 6 introduces refined and innovative mechanics that enhance both competitive depth and accessibility. The game features the Drive System, a new core mechanic that uses a Drive Gauge for executing powerful moves like Drive Impact, Drive Parry, and Overdrive specials. Players can choose between Classic, Modern, and Dynamic control schemes, catering to both veterans and newcomers. Additionally, mechanics like Perfect Parry, Punish Counter, and Wall Rush add layers of strategy, timing, and positioning to each fight.
Featured Characters:

Ryu

Fighter

Ryu is a disciplined and honorable martial artist who seeks strength and self-mastery through continuous training. Known for his iconic Hadoken and Shoryuken techniques, he uses a balanced fighting style rooted in Ansatsuken. In Street Fighter 6, Ryu appears older and wiser, embodying the role of a seasoned warrior passing on his knowledge. His calm demeanor and dedication to improvement make him a symbol of the Street Fighter series.

Juri

Fighter

Juri is a chaotic and aggressive fighter who thrives on violence and psychological torment. With her deadly Taekwondo-based style and the power of the Feng Shui Engine in her eye, she delivers fast, unpredictable attacks. In Street Fighter 6, she retains her sadistic personality and playful arrogance, often mocking her opponents during battle. Juri’s unpredictable nature and flashy moves make her one of the most dangerous and entertaining characters in the roster.

Game Tournaments:
The biggest esports tournaments for Street Fighter 6 in 2024 were the Esports World Cup in Riyadh and Evo 2024 in Las Vegas. Xiao Hai won the Esports World Cup, while Punk made history at Evo by becoming the first American in two decades to win a mainline Street Fighter title.
Mortal Kombat 1
NetherRealm Studios | September 19, 2023 | Microsoft WindowsPlayStation 5Nintendo SwitchPlaystation 4Xbox One
Mortal Kombat 1 is a soft reboot of the Mortal Kombat universe, following a reimagined timeline created by the god Liu Kang. The game emphasizes brutal, cinematic combat, includes the signature gory Fatalities the series is known for, and introduces the Kameo system, where a second character can assist you mid-battle.
Game Mechanics:
MK1 reboots the Mortal Kombat timeline with fresh mechanics while keeping its brutal core intact. The biggest addition is the Kameo Fighter System, which lets players call in assist characters during matches for combos, defense, or zoning. The game features a refined combo system, aerial combos, and breaker mechanics to interrupt enemy offense. It also retains cinematic Fatal Blows and Fatalities, delivering signature over-the-top violence.
Featured Heroes:

Scorpion

Fighter

Scorpion is a vengeful ninja specter wielding fire-based attacks and his iconic spear to pull enemies in. In MK1, he’s reimagined as a Lin Kuei warrior and brother to Sub-Zero, with a new backstory that adds depth. He excels in mid-range combat with teleporting slashes and fiery mix-ups. His gameplay blends aggression and mobility for high-damage rushdown.

LLiu Kang

Fighter

Liu Kang is the God of Fire and the creator of the new timeline in MK1, taking Raiden’s former role as protector. He combines traditional martial arts with divine fire and lightning attacks, making him a balanced and versatile fighter. His style includes rapid strikes, projectile pressure, and powerful zoning tools. As a central figure in the story, he’s both a narrative and gameplay powerhouse.

Game Tournaments:
The Pro Kompetition is the official global tournament circuit for MK1, organized by Warner Bros. Games and NetherRealm Studios. Players compete in various events to earn points, aiming to qualify for the Final Kombat World Championship.
