JD Gaming PLAYERS

Bai Jiahao AKA 369

369

Role: Top Lane

Team: JDG

About

Bai Jiahao is a Chinese top laner known for his adaptability and composure. He’s capable of playing both weakside tanks and dominant carry champions. 369 has consistently delivered strong performances in both domestic and international events. He was pivotal in JDG’s MSI 2023 win with his versatility. His intelligent laning and late-game decision-making elevate his team.

Achievements

As an MSI champion and dominant LPL laner, 369 is known for consistently winning lane and contributing heavily in teamfights.

Seo Jin-hyeok AKA Kanavi

About

Seo Jin-hyeok is a South Korean jungler who made a name for himself in China with JDG. Known for his cerebral jungle pathing, he blends precision with adaptability. After joining JDG in 2019, Kanavi quickly became a fan favorite for his ability to carry from the jungle. His synergy with his laners and objective control are elite. He played a major role in JDG's MSI and LPL victories.

Achievements

An LPL MVP and MSI champion, Kanavi is widely regarded as one of the smartest and most mechanically gifted junglers in the world.

Zhuo Ding AKA Knight

Knight

Role: MId Lane

Team: JDG

About

Zhuo Ding, better known as Knight, is a Chinese mid laner famed for his mechanics and game sense. He’s a key playmaker and teamfight specialist. Knight rose to fame with Top Esports before joining JDG in 2023. His consistency in lane and clutch performances in teamfights are hallmarks of his career. He was crowned MVP of MSI 2023.

Achievements

Knight was awarded the MSI 2023 MVP for his stellar mid lane control and is viewed as a franchise cornerstone of JDG.

Park Jae-hyuk AKA Ruler

About

Park Jae-hyuk is a veteran ADC from South Korea currently playing for JDG. He brings a decade of international experience and flawless positioning. Ruler won Worlds in 2017 with Samsung Galaxy and brought his veteran prowess to JDG in 2023. His late-game carry potential and clutch instincts remain unmatched. His leadership stabilized the JDG bot lane.

Achievements

A 2017 Worlds Champion and MSI 2023 winner, Ruler is one of the most accomplished ADCs in League history, revered for his consistency and elite-level play.

Ruler

Role: Bot Lane

Lou Yunfeng AKA Missing

Missing

Role: Support

Team: JDG

About

Lou Yunfeng is a Chinese support who plays a vital role in JDG’s structure. He excels at creating opportunities and enabling his teammates. Missing joined JDG after proving himself on Team WE and quickly meshed with the squad. Known for his smart roams and synergy with Ruler, he anchors the team’s vision and engage strategies. His decision-making has been key in tight matches.

Achievements

Celebrated for his intelligent engages and map awareness, Missing was key to JDG’s MSI 2023 victory and remains a tactical backbone.
