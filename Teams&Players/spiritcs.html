About

A Russian esports team known for its strategic gameplay and rising prominence in CS2. Team Spirit has made waves in the global scene with its disciplined approach and adaptability. With a growing fanbase and a dedicated lineup, they are quickly becoming one of the most formidable teams in CS2.

History

Established in 2015, Team Spirit has steadily climbed the ranks of competitive CS, gaining international recognition with their victories in CS:GO. Their transition to CS2 has been seamless, and they continue to impress with their strategic depth. Team Spirit’s rise to the upper echelons of CS2 is marked by their resilience and strong team synergy.
