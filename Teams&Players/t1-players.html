T1 PLAYERS

Lee Sang-hyeok AKA Faker

Faker

Role: Mid Lane

Team: T1

About

Lee Sang-hyeok from South Korea, known globally as Faker, is widely regarded as the greatest League of Legends player of all time. His unmatched skill, mental fortitude, and leadership have kept T1 at the top for over a decade. Faker debuted in 2013 and instantly became a global sensation with his Zed outplay. He has carried T1 through every era of League with mechanical brilliance and a deep champion pool. Even a decade later, his dominance continues to inspire the global LoL community.

Achievements

Faker is a 3× Worlds Champion, 2× MSI winner, and has earned 10 LCK titles. He is celebrated for his ability to clutch high-pressure matches and remains a symbol of the game’s global legacy.

Choi Woo-je AKA Zeus

About

Choi Woo-je is a rising top laner from South Korea who became a breakout star with T1. Known for his fearless laning and high damage output, he quickly rose to international prominence. Zeus joined T1 in 2021 and rapidly became one of the most dominant top laners in the LCK. His solo kills and lane pressure created consistent leads for the team. His MVP performance at Worlds 2023 solidified him as one of the game’s brightest young stars.

Achievements

Named the 2023 Worlds MVP, Zeus was pivotal in T1’s championship run, showcasing exceptional solo lane play and impact in teamfights.

Mun Hyeon-jun AKA Oner

Oner

Role: Jungle

Team: T1

About

Mun Hyeon-jun is a young South Korean jungler renowned for his aggressive early-game control and intelligent pathing. He is known for both his mechanics and leadership despite his youth. Oner made his debut in 2021 and quickly became T1’s engine, dictating the pace of games through proactive jungle play. He excels at controlling objectives and reading the map. His presence was vital to T1's 2023 Worlds run.

Achievements

A 2023 Worlds Champion and multiple LCK titleholder, Oner’s ability to dictate tempo makes him one of Korea’s premier junglers.

Lee Min-hyeong AKA Gumayusi

About

Lee Min-hyeong, known as Gumayusi, is T1’s high-caliber ADC from South Korea. He brings incredible mechanical skill and composure to the bot lane. Joining T1’s main roster in 2021, Gumayusi earned respect for his sharp laning phase and devastating late-game damage. His synergy with Keria has formed one of the strongest bot lanes in the world. His confidence in teamfights is a cornerstone of T1's success.

Achievements

As a key performer in the 2023 Worlds, Gumayusi’s precision in positioning and damage carry were crucial to T1’s victory.

Ryu Min-seok AKA Keria

Keria

Role: Support

Team: T1

About

Ryu Min-seok is a South Korean support player famous for his creative champion pool and high IQ macro decisions. He’s known for his vision control and clutch shotcalling. Keria joined T1 in 2021 and quickly emerged as one of the world’s most innovative and reliable supports. His adaptability and synergy with Gumayusi set the bot lane apart. His incredible engages and saves won critical fights at Worlds 2023.

Achievements

Keria’s clutch performances and game-saving engages were instrumental in T1’s Worlds 2023 win, making him one of the world’s most respected supports.
