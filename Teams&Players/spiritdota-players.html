Team Spirit PLAYERS

Illya Mulyarchuk AKA Yatoro

Yatoro

Role: Carry

Team: Team Spirit

About

Illya Mulyarchuk, from Ukraine, is renowned for his versatile hero pool and composure under pressure. Known for excelling in clutch moments, he quickly became a core player for Team Spirit after joining in 2020. His flexible approach to different roles has made him a pivotal part of their roster. Yatoro began his professional career in 2020, joining Team Spirit and quickly establishing himself as one of the most promising carry players in the scene. His skill with multiple heroes, combined with a calm demeanor under pressure, made him essential to Team Spirit's rise to the top, especially in their TI wins.

Achievements

Two time TI Champion (2021, 2023), with a diverse hero pool that has been instrumental in securing victories. Yatoro is regarded as one of the top carry players in Dota 2, with a strong presence in high-stakes matches.

Denis Sigitov AKA Larl

About

Denis Sigitov, from Russia, is the midlaner for Team Spirit. Larl is known for his sharp decision-making and high-impact plays in the mid-game. His ability to consistently perform under pressure has made him one of the most reliable players in the competitive scene. Larl transitioned from various regional teams to Team Spirit, where his growth as a midlaner has been significant. His game sense and mechanics allowed Team Spirit to dominate in the mid-lane, playing a crucial role in their TI12 win.

Achievements

Key player in Team Spirit’s TI12 victory, with exceptional midlane control and precision. Larl’s rise to prominence has solidified him as one of the top midlaners in Dota 2.

Magomed Khalilov AKA Collapse

Collapse

Role: Offlaner

Team: Team Spirit

About

Magomed Khalilov, from Russia, is an offlaner known for his game-changing initiations and aggressive style of play. Collapse has become a key player in setting up pivotal team fights, using his deep understanding of the game to gain strategic advantages. His leadership in crucial moments is essential to Team Spirit’s success. Collapse has been with Team Spirit since 2020, contributing his unique offlane strategies that often turn the tide of battles.
