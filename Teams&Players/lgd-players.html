Guo Xuanang AKA Shiro

Shiro

Role: Carry

Team: LGD Gaming

About

Guo Xuanang, known as shiro, is a rising Chinese carry player with a powerful presence in the game. His mechanical precision and farming efficiency have quickly earned him a reputation as a top-tier carry in Dota 2. Shiro is known for his ability to make impactful plays when it matters most, particularly during high-stakes moments. Shiro's talent was first recognized during his time in the Chinese Dota 2 scene, where he gained attention for his exceptional carry performances. His rise within LGD Gaming has been marked by significant growth, and he has played a crucial role in securing the team’s success at TI12.

Achievements

Shiro has become a key carry player for LGD Gaming, showing his consistency in high-pressure moments. His skill and impact on the game have solidified his place as one of China’s top Dota 2 talents.

About

Achievements
