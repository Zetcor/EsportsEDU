Sentinels PLAYERS

Tyson Ngo AKA TenZ

TenZ

Role: Duelist

Team: Sentinels

About

Tyson Ngo, known as TenZ, is a Canadian Valorant prodigy, widely regarded as one of the best mechanical players in the scene. His incredible aim and versatility on the roster have made him an irreplaceable asset for Sentinels. After making his mark in CS:GO, TenZ transitioned to Valorant and quickly became one of the top players in North America. TenZ was loaned to Sentinels during Masters Reykjavik 2021, where he helped them secure the championship. His high-skill ceiling and adaptability have made him a top contender in all major tournaments.

Achievements

TenZ helped lead Sentinels to their VCT Masters Reykjavik 2021 Championship win and has since continued to be one of the most consistent and standout players in Valorant.

Gustavo Rossi AKA Sacy

About

Gustavo Rossi, known as Sacy, is a Brazilian veteran who plays as a controller for Sentinels. He brings years of tactical expertise and leadership to the team, having previously been a key player for the Brazilian squad, FURIA. Sacy’s consistency and clutch moments have earned him recognition in the competitive Valorant scene. Sacy transitioned to Sentinels in 2022 after an illustrious run with FURIA. His deep understanding of the game and ability to lead have made him a valuable asset to the team.

Achievements

Sacy helped lead Sentinels to the 2022 VCT Championship and has been known for his smart decision-making and ability to control critical areas of the map.

Bryan Luna AKA pANcada

pANcada

Role: Initiator

Team: Sentinels

About

Bryan Luna, known as pANcada, is a Brazilian player and one of Sentinels’ key controllers. He has a reputation for strong positioning and exceptional utility usage in high-stakes games. pANcada’s tactical insight and calm demeanor under pressure make him a crucial component of Sentinels' strategy. pANcada’s aggressive playstyle, paired with his methodical approach to utility, has made him a fan favorite. His work ethic and ability to adapt to different roles help Sentinels dominate the regional league.

Achievements

pANcada’s tactical prowess helped Sentinels secure their 2022 VCT Championship win. He remains one of the strongest and most reliable controllers in Valorant.

Zachary Patrone AKA Zekken

About

Zachary Patrone, known as zekken, is a rising star from the United States, known for his explosive performances and duelist capabilities. His sharp mechanics and aggressive playstyle make him a dangerous player in 1v1 situations, especially in high-pressure moments. Zekken’s breakout performances during the 2021 and 2022 VCT seasons earned him a spot in Sentinels, where he quickly became one of their key players. His impressive mechanics and ability to carry Sentinels in critical rounds make him a top duelist.

Achievements

Zekken has delivered several standout moments for Sentinels, particularly with his lethal duelist performances and clutch plays, making him an essential part of their success.
