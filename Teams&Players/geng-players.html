Gen.G PLAYERS

Choi Hyeon-joon AKA Doran

Doran

Role: Top Lane

Team: Gen.G

About

Choi Hyeon-joon is a South Korean top laner known for his calm, reliable playstyle. As a key part of Gen.G's structure, he excels at absorbing pressure and enabling his team. His adaptability and consistency make him a cornerstone of their success. Began career with DragonX in 2019 before joining Gen.G. Known for his Ornn and Gnar proficiency in teamfights. Won LCK titles in 2022 and 2023 with Gen.G.

Achievements

A top-tier LCK top laner, Doran is praised for his steady performances in high-pressure matches. His ability to neutralize opponents and contribute in teamfights has been vital to Gen.G's domestic dominance.

Han Wang-ho AKA Peanut

About

Han Wang-ho, nicknamed Peanut, is a veteran jungler with a clever, aggressive pathing style. Since debuting in 2015, he's been a standout in Korea for his adaptability and leadership. His experience brings stability to Gen.G's early-game strategies. Rose to fame on ROX Tigers during their legendary 2016 season. Played for SKT T1 before joining Gen.G in 2022. Holds record for most LCK jungler MVPs in history.

Achievements

A multi-time LCK champion and MSI contender, Peanut is renowned for his clutch plays and versatility. His ability to control the jungle tempo has cemented his legacy as one of Korea's greatest junglers.

Jeong Ji-hoon AKA Chovy

Chovy

Role:Mid Lane

Team: Gen.G

About

Jeong Ji-hoon, known as Chovy, is a mechanical genius in the mid lane. His near-flawless laning and sharp decision-making make him a nightmare for opponents. Chovy's consistency and carry potential are unmatched in the LCK. Gained fame for his 100+ KDA on Griffin in 2018. Known as "the perfect laner" for his CS dominance. Finally won LCK titles after joining Gen.G in 2022.

Achievements

A frequent LCK MVP, Chovy dominates with elite mechanics and teamfight prowess. His performances have solidified him as one of the best mid laners in the world, capable of single-handedly swinging games.

Kim Su-hwan AKA Peyz

About

Kim Su-hwan is a rising ADC prodigy with exceptional positioning and aggression. His fearless playstyle and rapid development have made him a standout in Gen.G's bot lane. Peyz's potential marks him as a future superstar. Gen.G's youngest player at 17 when he debuted. Won LCK in his rookie split (Spring 2023). Known for his Aphelios and Zeri performances.

Achievements

Recognized as a breakout talent, Peyz has shined in both LCK and international play. His high damage output and clutch performances have already earned him praise as one of Korea's next great ADCs.

Yoo Hwan-joong AKA Delight

Delight

Role: Support

Team: Gen.G

About

Yoo Hwan-joong is a selfless support known for his vision control and playmaking. His synergy with Peyz and calm decision-making are crucial to Gen.G's success. Delight's ability to read the game elevates his team's macro play. Formerly played for Fredit BRION before Gen.G. Specializes in engage supports like Rakan and Nautilus. Won LCK Summer 2023 with perfect synergy with Peyz.

Achievements

A key part of Gen.G's strategic depth, Delight's shotcalling and roams have been instrumental in their victories. His consistency in high-stakes matches proves his value as a top-tier support.
