About

Team Liquid is a Western European powerhouse known for its consistency and team-oriented gameplay. The squad thrives on strategic depth, with each player contributing to the team's coordinated playstyle. Their focus on teamwork and versatility has consistently placed them among the top contenders in international Dota 2 tournaments.

History

Team Liquid has been a staple of the Dota 2 scene, with regular top finishes in international tournaments. Their consistent performances have earned them a reputation for stability and excellence, culminating in multiple podium finishes at major events. At TI12, Team Liquid secured a runner-up position, cementing their place as one of the world's elite Dota 2 teams.
