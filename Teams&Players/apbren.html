Location: Philippines

About

AP Bren is a powerhouse Mobile Legends team from the Philippines known for their strategic depth and mechanical precision. Renowned for their discipline and team coordination, they consistently dominate Southeast Asian competitions. Their win at M5 solidified their legacy as one of MLBB's most iconic teams.

History

Formerly known as Bren Esports, the team rebranded to AP Bren and has remained a top-tier contender in both regional and international play. They previously won the M2 World Championship and reclaimed their world title with the M5 victory, showcasing growth and adaptability across seasons. Their roster features some of the most experienced and decorated players in the MLBB scene.
