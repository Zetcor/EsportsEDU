About

ONIC Esports is a leading Indonesian MLBB team known for their sharp early-game tempo, aggressive skirmishing, and tactical team coordination. Their high mechanical ceiling and disciplined map control made them strong contenders at M5. As MPL ID champions, they carried high expectations into the tournament and delivered a deep playoff run.

History

ONIC Esports has long been a dominant force in MPL Indonesia, claiming multiple titles and developing a reputation for cultivating elite talent.
