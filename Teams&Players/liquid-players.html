Michael V AKA miCKe

miCKe

Role: Carry

Team: Team Liquid

About

Michael "miCKe" Vu, from Sweden, is a versatile player known for his adaptability in multiple roles. His ability to perform across various positions allows Team Liquid to execute diverse strategies. MiCKe’s flexibility makes him one of the most well-rounded players in the Dota 2 scene. MiCKe’s career includes stints in other high-level organizations before joining Team Liquid, where he quickly established himself as a core player. His contributions in both offlane and carry roles have been vital in securing Team Liquid's international success.

Achievements

MiCKe has been a key figure in Team Liquid’s strong international finishes, including their runner-up spot at TI12. His impact across multiple roles showcases his diverse skillset and adaptability.

Michał Jankowski AKA Nisha

About

Michał "Nisha" Jankowski is a top-tier Polish midlaner, known for his precise mechanics and ability to dominate lanes. He is celebrated for his strategic thinking and flexibility in adapting to different metas. Nisha’s deep hero pool and quick decision-making make him a major force in the mid-lane. Nisha has been one of the most consistent midlaners in the Dota 2 competitive scene, earning recognition for his top-tier performances in both international and regional events. His time with Team Liquid has been marked by impressive individual plays and a significant contribution to their team success.

Achievements

Nisha’s numerous accolades include top finishes at major tournaments, including a runner-up finish at TI12. His ability to play high-impact heroes has made him a key player in Team Liquid’s success.
