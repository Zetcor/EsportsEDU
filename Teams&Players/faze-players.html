FaZe PLAYERS

Finn Andersen AKA karrigan

karrigan

Role: IGL

Team: FaZe

About

Karrigan is a Danish in-game leader known for his longevity, experience, and ability to unite diverse rosters. He is one of the few players to have remained at the top level for over a decade, guiding teams across multiple eras of Counter-Strike.

Achievements

Karrigan has led FaZe Clan to victories at some of the most prestigious tournaments in CS history, including IEM Katowice, ESL Pro League, and PGL Major Antwerp 2022. His ability to adapt his leadership to different teammates and situations has earned him recognition as one of the greatest IGLs in CS.

Helvijs Smits AKA broky

About

Broky is a Latvian AWPer who joined FaZe as a relatively unknown player and quickly evolved into one of the most dependable clutch players in the game. He is known for his composure, excellent timing, and ability to turn around difficult rounds.

Achievements

Broky has been a critical component in FaZe’s top finishes and major victories, including high-pressure moments in playoff stages. His reputation as a calm and effective AWPer has made him a consistent threat to opponents at the highest level.

Håvard Nygaard AKA rain

rain

Role: Rifler

Team: FaZe

About

Rain is a Norwegian rifler and the longest-standing member of FaZe Clan. Known for his consistency and work ethic, he has remained a key part of the team through numerous roster changes and continues to deliver strong performances.

Achievements

Rain was named MVP of the PGL Major Antwerp 2022 and played a central role in FaZe’s championship run. His steady presence and impact rifling have contributed to the team’s success for several years.

Robin Kool AKA ropz

About

Ropz is an Estonian rifler recognized for his methodical, intelligent playstyle. Formerly a semi-professional Call of Duty player, he switched to CS and rose rapidly, known for his discipline and precision.

Achievements

Since joining FaZe, ropz has helped the team win multiple tournaments, consistently delivering in clutch moments. His clean mechanics and smart decisions have made him one of the most respected riflers in CS2.

David Čerňanský AKA frozen

frozen

Role: Rifler

Team: FaZe

About

Frozen is a Slovakian player who was considered a prodigy from a young age. He gained early experience with mousesports before joining FaZe, where he brought youthful energy and raw firepower to an experienced roster.

Achievements

Frozen has made a major impact with his aggressive and technically skilled play, quickly becoming one of FaZe’s top fraggers. His addition has helped the team remain competitive at the top level.
