Leonid Vishnyakov AKA chopper

chopper

Role: IGL

Team: Spirit

About

Chopper is a Russian in-game leader known for his strategic mindset and ability to stay composed under pressure. He has led Team Spirit with maturity and adaptability, helping shape the team’s identity as a rising powerhouse in CS2.

Achievements

Led Team Spirit to the finals of IEM Katowice 2025. Known for his strong leadership and ability to adapt strategies on the fly, chopper’s role in Team Spirit’s success cannot be overstated.

Boris Vorobiev AKA magixx

About

Magixx is a Russian rifler who has been a steady and reliable presence in Team Spirit since joining in 2019. His calm, calculated approach to the game brings balance to the team in both high-stakes matches and long tournaments.

Achievements

Consistent performer in major tournaments, contributing to Team Spirit’s rise in the CS2 competitive scene. His steady play has earned him recognition as a key player on the roster.
