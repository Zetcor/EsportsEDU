ONIC PLAYERS

Kairi Rayosdelsol

Kairi

Role: Core

Team: ONIC PH

About

Kairi Rayosdelsol, originally from the Philippines, plays as ONIC’s jungler and is known for his sharp decision-making and refined mechanics. He was the first Filipino import in MPL Indonesia. Kairi made history by transitioning to the Indonesian league and becoming a key piece in ONIC's rise to international prominence. His experience and adaptability allowed him to outmaneuver global opponents and lead ONIC through M5’s intense playoff battles.

Achievements

MPL ID Champion; M5 Runner-up; first Filipino import to thrive in a foreign league.

Muhammad Satrya Sanubari AKA Butss

About

Muhammad Satrya “Butss” Sanubari is an EXP laner from Indonesia known for his calm laning style and strong zone control during teamfights. Butss has been a staple of ONIC’s lineup, consistently providing front-line stability and engaging with perfect timing. His smart positioning and hero flexibility were crucial during ONIC’s run to the M5 Grand Finals.

Achievements

MPL ID Champion; M5 Runner-up with consistent tank and utility performances.

Thomas Obadja AKA SamoHt

SamoHt

Role: Roam

Team: ONIC PH

About

Thomas Obadja, better known as SamoHt, is an Indonesian roamer praised for his map awareness and aggressive initiations. SamoHt’s ability to create space and initiate clutch teamfights was instrumental in ONIC’s tournament journey. His synergy with jungler Kairi helped establish early map control in multiple series.

Achievements

M5 Runner-up; key playmaker with international experience.

Calvin Winata AKA CW

About

Calvin Winata is a gold laner from Indonesia known for his consistent farming and lethal late-game damage output. CW brings a steady and reliable presence to the bot lane. During M5, his positioning and patience allowed him to deliver critical damage in teamfights, often turning games in ONIC’s favor.

Achievements

MPL ID Champion; M5 Runner-up with clutch carry performances.

Gilang AKA SANZ

SANZ

Role: Mid Lane

Team: ONIC PH

About

Gilang “SANZ” is an Indonesian mid laner known for his exceptional game sense, versatile hero pool, and consistent damage output. A longtime cornerstone of ONIC Esports, SANZ has transitioned from a hyper-carry role to a more versatile mid lane position, showcasing both aggressive burst mages and utility picks. His adaptability and experience were critical during M5, where he frequently dictated teamfights and neutral objective control. SANZ’s calm under pressure and ability to turn the tide in close matches made him a key figure in ONIC's journey to the Grand Finals.

Achievements

MPL ID Champion; M5 Runner-up; known for clutch performances and mid lane dominance.
