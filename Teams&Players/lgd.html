About

LGD Gaming is a Chinese esports powerhouse known for its deep history in Dota 2. The team has consistently performed at the highest level, maintaining a reputation for strong team coordination and individual skill. With a legacy of top-tier finishes, LGD remains one of the most respected names in the international Dota 2 scene.

History

LGD Gaming has a long-standing presence in Dota 2, with consistent top finishes in major tournaments. Their performance at TI12 earned them a 3rd place finish, adding to their storied history of near-misses and podium finishes. With an ever-evolving roster, LGD continues to be a key contender at each iteration of The International, showcasing their adaptability and skill.
