ECHO PLAYERS

Karl Nepomuceno AKA KarlTzy

KarlTzy

Role: Core

Team: ECHO PH

About

Karl Nepomuceno is a jungler from the Philippines renowned for his mechanical mastery and past world championship experience. A two-time world champion (M2 and M4), KarlTzy made history by winning titles with two different teams. At M4, he redefined his jungle role with calculated aggression and versatile picks, leading ECHO’s early-game dominance.

Achievements

M2 and M4 World Champion; first MLBB player to win two World Championships.

Sanford Marin Vinuya

About

Sanford Marin Vinuya is an EXP laner from the Philippines known for his clutch flanks and durable teamfight presence. As one of the youngest starters in the M4 playoffs, Sanford stepped up massively, showcasing impeccable timing and teamfight discipline. His performances against top EXP laners proved pivotal to ECHO’s upper bracket dominance.

Achievements

M4 World Champion; breakout EXP laner with game-winning plays.

Alston Pabico AKA Sanji

Sanji

Role: Mid Lane

Team: ECHO PH

About

Alston Pabico is a mid laner from the Philippines praised for his burst damage execution and mature game sense. Sanji emerged as one of the most impactful rookies of the tournament, excelling on utility and burst mages. His synergy with KarlTzy and support from Yawi made ECHO’s mid-control one of the strongest at M4.

Achievements

M4 World Champion; standout mid laner in his international debut.

Benedict Gonzales AKA Bennyqt

About

Benedict Gonzales is a gold laner from the Philippines known for his deadly positioning and late-game carry power. Bennyqt’s ability to deliver consistent damage in chaotic fights was unmatched. In the Grand Finals, he delivered MVP-worthy performances, shredding through defenses and anchoring team victories.

Achievements

M4 Finals MVP; M4 World Champion; elite gold lane finisher.

Tristan Cabrera AKA Yawi

Yawi

Role: Roam

Team: ONIC PH

About

Tristan Cabrera is a roamer from the Philippines recognized for his fearless engages and charismatic leadership. Known for his flashy and unpredictable plays, Yawi controlled the tempo in many of ECHO’s fights. His synergy with KarlTzy and shotcalling were essential in orchestrating clutch plays throughout M4.

Achievements

M4 World Champion; fan-favorite roamer with high-impact performance.
