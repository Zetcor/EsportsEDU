About

A Ukrainian esports powerhouse renowned for its dominance in the Counter-Strike scene. NAVI has earned a reputation for producing top-tier players and consistently competing at the highest level. Their synergy and strategic playstyle have made them a force to be reckoned with in every major tournament.

History

Established in 2009, NAVI has secured multiple major titles, with a legacy of producing world-class talent. The team has been a mainstay in the global esports scene, with top finishes in major CS2 tournaments and a history of exceptional players and coaches.
