Erick Santos AKA Aspas

Aspas

Role: Duelist

Team: LOUD

About

Erick Santos, also known as Aspas, hails from Brazil and is one of the most consistent duelists in Valorant. Known for his pinpoint accuracy and fearless gameplay, Aspas has developed a reputation for making plays under pressure. His remarkable ability to close out rounds has made him a fan favorite and a key figure in LOUD’s success. Aspas rose to prominence with his exceptional individual skill, known for his incredible aim and clutch moments. His gameplay was a major factor in LOUD’s victory, where his fragging power led the charge in critical rounds.

Achievements

Aspas played a pivotal role in securing LOUD's VCT Champions 2022 victory, solidifying himself as one of Brazil's premier duelists. His ability to perform under pressure made him a top player in the region.

Matias Delipetro AKA Saadhak

About

Matias Delipetro, known as saadhak, is an Argentine professional who serves as the in-game leader (IGL) for LOUD. His leadership extends beyond just shotcalling; he is known for his calm, calculated approach to the game. With a wealth of experience from various Brazilian and international teams, Saadhak has been a guiding force behind LOUD’s rise. Saadhak’s leadership skills have guided LOUD through numerous competitive seasons. His understanding of the game and strategic decision-making make him an invaluable asset to the team. His leadership played a critical role in LOUD’s 2022 success.

Achievements

As IGL, Saadhak was instrumental in guiding LOUD to VCT Champions 2022 victory, helping the team execute flawless strategies throughout the tournament.

Felipe Basso AKA Less

Less

Role: Controller

Team: LOUD

About

Felipe Basso, known as Less, is a Brazilian talent whose mechanical skill and game sense have placed him at the forefront of Valorant’s competitive scene. Despite being relatively young, Less has shown incredible maturity in his gameplay, earning a spot on the renowned LOUD roster. His rise as a player has been meteoric, with a growing fanbase as a result of his standout performances. Less quickly became one of Brazil's most exciting talents, drawing attention with his standout performances in both regional and international events. His development as a player was rapid, and he became an essential part of LOUD's core roster.

Achievements

Less’s breakout year helped LOUD secure major wins, including their VCT Champions title in 2022, where he played a crucial role in their success.
