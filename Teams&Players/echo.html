About

ECHO is a dominant Filipino MLBB team known for their explosive mechanical skill, aggressive map pressure, and dynamic teamfighting. Their synergy and raw talent allowed them to dismantle top-tier teams en route to their M4 victory. With a young, fearless core, ECHO redefined aggressive play in the global MLBB scene.

History

Rising through MPL PH as a fast-developing squad, ECHO sharpened their identity in 2022 before peaking at M4. Their grand finals 4-0 sweep of Blacklist International shocked the world and cemented them as a new powerhouse in international MLBB.
