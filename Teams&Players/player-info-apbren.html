PLAYERS

Der

Role: EXP Lane

Team: Onic PH

About

Landher "Der" Mendoza San Gabriel, born on February 4, 1999, is a seasoned Filipino MLBB professional currently playing as the EXP Laner for AP.Bren. Known for his adaptability and consistent performance, Der has been an integral part of several prominent teams throughout his career. He began his professional journey with Cignal Ultra in November 2020, then moved to TNC Pro Team in July 2021. His tenure with ONIC Philippines from July 2022 to June 2023 was marked by significant growth, after which he joined ONIC Arsenals until August 2024. Subsequently, he played for Lazy Esports before being acquired by AP.Bren on February 12, 2025. At 26 years old, Der stands as the most experienced player on AP.Bren's roster, bringing a wealth of knowledge and stability to the team's EXP Lane. His journey reflects a dedication to excellence and a deep understanding of the game's dynamics, making him a valuable asset in the competitive MLBB scene.

Achievements

  • M5 World Championship (2023): As a member of AP.Bren, Der played a pivotal role in the team's victory at the M5 World Championship, where they defeated ONIC Esports Indonesia 4-3 in a thrilling grand final held at the Rizal Memorial Coliseum in Manila. This win marked AP.Bren's second M-Series title, making them the first team to achieve this feat.
  • MPL Philippines Season 12 Champion (2023): Der was part of the AP.Bren roster that clinched the MPL PH Season 12 title, overcoming Blacklist International with a 4-1 scoreline in the grand finals. This victory ended a five-season championship drought for the team
  • Southeast Asian Games Gold Medalist (2023): Representing the Philippines, Der and the national team secured the gold medal in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang at the 32nd Southeast Asian Games held in Cambodia
