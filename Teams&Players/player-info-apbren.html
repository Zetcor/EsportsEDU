AP BREN PLAYERS

Angelo Arcangel AKA Pheww

Pheww

Role: Mid Lane

Team: AP Bren

About

Angelo Arcangel from the Philippines is a mid laner known for his leadership, shotcalling, and composure in high-pressure matches. He is one of the most respected veterans in the MLBB pro scene. Pheww has been part of the Bren core since their M2 victory and is one of the few players to win multiple MLBB world titles. His deep hero pool and ability to control tempo in mid-lane battles are key to AP Bren’s success. He serves as the team captain, orchestrating rotations and fights with precision.

Achievements

Two-time MLBB World Champion (M2, M5); veteran leader and cornerstone of AP Bren’s championship runs.

David Canon AKA FlapTzy

About

David Canon, from the Philippines, is an EXP laner famous for his clutch plays and strong laning mechanics. He brings youthful energy and discipline to the frontline. FlapTzy rose to fame as a breakout star during the M2 Championship and has since remained a consistent performer in global tournaments. His understanding of macro and timely rotations make him invaluable in teamfights. He continues to evolve his role, providing flexibility and impact in the EXP lane.

Achievements

M2 and M5 World Champion; known for his durability and late-game teamfight presence.

Kyle Sayson AKA KyleTzy

KyleTzy

Role: Core

Team: AP Bren

About

Kyle Sayson from the Philippines is a jungler renowned for his efficient farming and objective-focused gameplay. A rising star, he’s praised for his poise and intelligence. Joining AP Bren as a successor to the original "Tzy" junglers, KyleTzy quickly established himself as a formidable talent. His control over jungle tempo and calm under pressure helped AP Bren reclaim their world title. He is celebrated for his mature decision-making despite being a newer face in the roster. Games: Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

Achievements

M5 World Champion; breakout jungler with consistent tournament MVP-level performances.

Marco Requitiano AKA Super Marco

About

Marco Requitiano is a gold laner from the Philippines known for his sharp positioning and late-game carry potential. He plays a crucial damage-dealing role for the team. Super Marco made waves with his high KDA performances and smart teamfight execution. He consistently delivers in key games, often topping damage charts while maintaining low deaths. His chemistry with support player Owgwen is vital to AP Bren’s bot lane success.

Achievements

M5 World Champion; rising star among MLBB gold laners with a reputation for clutch performances.

Rowgien Unigo AKA Owgwen

Owgwen

Role: Roam

Team: AP Bren

About

Rowgien Unigo from the Philippines is a support player known for his aggressive roam plays and excellent synergy with the team’s tempo. He is praised for his vision and engage timings. Owgwen has developed into one of Southeast Asia’s top supports, often initiating fights and setting up objectives for his team. His map presence and playmaking instincts allow AP Bren to control the pace of the game. He plays a key role in dictating early-to-mid game momentum.

Achievements

M5 World Champion; regarded as a top-tier MLBB support with game-altering setups.
