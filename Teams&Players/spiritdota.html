#SPIRITED

Location: Serbia

About

Eastern European powerhouse known for strategic depth and resilience. With a history of top-tier performances, Team Spirit solidified their legacy at TI12 with their second Aegis of Champions. The team's cohesive style and adaptability make them a formidable force in the Dota 2 world.

History

Team Spirit achieved their second Aegis of Champions at TI12, confirming their status as one of the top teams in Dota 2. Their victory came after a series of dominant performances and high-caliber teamwork. With exceptional drafting and individual prowess, they have cemented their place in Dota 2 history.
