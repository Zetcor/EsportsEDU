NAVI PLAYERS

Valerij Vakhovsjkyj AKA b1t

b1t

Role: Rifler

Team: NAVI

About

B1t is a Ukrainian professional player who quickly rose through NAVI’s academy system to become one of the team’s most consistent riflers. Known for his disciplined approach, sharp aim, and calm mindset, he earned a permanent spot on the main roster after proving himself in high-stakes matches. His playing style emphasizes reliability, which makes him a cornerstone of NAVI’s core lineup.

Achievements

B1t has been instrumental in NAVI’s major tournament victories, including the PGL Major Stockholm, and has consistently delivered standout performances in crucial rounds. His accuracy and cool-headedness under pressure have earned him a reputation as one of the most dependable players on the global stage.

Aleksi Virolainen AKA Aleksib

About

Aleksib is a Finnish in-game leader known for his methodical and team-focused leadership. He began his career with Finnish team ENCE and later played for top-tier international teams before joining NAVI. His communication skills, calm presence, and tactical vision make him one of the most respected leaders in competitive CS.

Achievements

As a captain, Aleksib has guided multiple teams to deep runs in major tournaments, including ENCE’s finals appearance at IEM Katowice. With NAVI, he has brought structure and strategy that helped the team secure high placements and maintain competitive consistency against top-level opponents.

Justinas Lekavičius AKA jL

jL

Role: Rifler

Team: NAVI

About

JL is a Lithuanian rifler known for his aggressive, high-energy playstyle. His passion and emotional presence on stage bring intensity to NAVI’s gameplay. Starting from smaller teams, he worked his way into top-tier competition through relentless work and eye-catching performances.

Achievements

JL was named the MVP of the PGL Major Copenhagen 2025, where his confidence and aim helped lead NAVI to a major championship. His ability to take initiative and deliver clutch rounds under pressure has made him a fan favorite and an invaluable asset to the team.

Mihai Ivan AKA iM

About

iM is a Romanian rifler known for his adaptable playstyle and team-first mindset. He gained international attention with his standout performances on lesser-known teams before making the leap to NAVI. He is calm, focused, and highly versatile in both support and aggressive roles.

Achievements

iM has contributed to NAVI’s success through his ability to perform across multiple roles. His consistent impact during important matches has helped the team maintain composure and adaptability in challenging tournament situations.

Ihor Zhdanov AKA w0nderful

w0nderful

Role: Awper

Team: NAVI

About

W0nderful is a Ukrainian AWPer widely regarded as one of the most promising young talents in CS2. With a composed demeanor and sharp instincts, he quickly transitioned into high-level play and became a key figure in NAVI’s game plan. His performances reflect maturity beyond his years.

Achievements

W0nderful played a vital role in NAVI’s PGL Major Copenhagen 2025 victory, consistently delivering high-impact AWP rounds. His ability to hold down key areas of the map and win clutch situations made him a standout performer throughout the tournament.
