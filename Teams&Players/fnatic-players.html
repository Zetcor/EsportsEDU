FNATIC PLAYERS

Nikita Sirmitev AKA Derke

Derke

Role: Duelist

Team: FNATIC

About

Nikita Sirmitev, from Finland, is a highly skilled duelist known for his aggressive playstyle and precise aim. He plays a key role in FNATIC’s offensive strategies and excels at opening up rounds for his team. Derke transitioned from CS:GO into Valorant and quickly became one of Europe’s top duelists. His raw mechanical skill, combined with his ability to clutch under pressure, has made him an indispensable member of FNATIC. As a cornerstone of their offense, Derke’s performances helped FNATIC secure their 2023 VCT Championship.

Achievements

Derke played a pivotal role in FNATIC’s VCT 2023 victory, earning multiple MVP awards across tournaments. His mechanical prowess and consistency make him a top-tier player, with many considering him one of the best duelists in the world.

Jake Howlett AKA Boaster

About

Jake Howlett from the UK is the in-game leader (IGL) of FNATIC, known for his tactical brilliance and leadership. He is responsible for setting the team’s strategies and directing them through critical moments. Boaster has been an integral part of FNATIC since the team’s inception, guiding them to become one of Europe’s most successful teams. His leadership style blends analytical thinking with bold, aggressive decisions that often catch opponents off guard. Under his direction, FNATIC achieved great success, including their 2023 VCT title.

Achievements

Boaster’s leadership was instrumental in FNATIC’s 2023 VCT Championship win. As IGL, his decision-making and innovative strategies helped guide the team to the top of the Valorant world.

Emir Ali Beder AKA Alfajer

Alfajer

Role: Duelist

Team: FNATIC

About

Emir Ali Beder, from Turkey, is known for his versatile gameplay and ability to dominate the mid-game. His role often sees him anchoring critical rounds, making impactful plays at key moments. Alfajer’s adaptability and sharp decision-making make him one of the most reliable players on FNATIC. He’s known for stepping up when the team needs him most, often securing crucial rounds in high-pressure situations. His MVP-worthy performances in 2023 helped FNATIC secure the VCT Championship.

Achievements

Alfajer was honored with VCT MVP in 2023 for his stellar performances, making game-changing plays. His ability to perform under pressure has earned him a reputation as one of the top players in the Valorant scene.

Leo Jannesson

About

Leo Jannesson, from Sweden, is an expert in the support and initiator roles, providing essential utility and playmaking for his team. His sharp game sense helps create openings for his teammates. Leo’s strategic mind and support play are key to FNATIC’s success. As an initiator, he creates space for the team to execute strategies while providing vital utility in team fights. His contributions to FNATIC’s 2023 championship victory were crucial, as he maintained a steady presence throughout.

Achievements

Leo has become a key player for FNATIC, excelling in support roles and providing necessary utility to control the map. His contributions to FNATIC’s success are often understated but crucial to their victories.

Timofey Khromov AKA Chronicle

Chronicle

Role: Controller

Team: FNATIC

About

Timofey Khromov from Russia brings a wealth of experience and explosive firepower to FNATIC’s roster. Known for his ability to perform in high-stakes matches, he’s often relied upon to make decisive plays. Chronicle has been a consistent performer for FNATIC since joining the team, bringing his raw mechanical talent and calm under pressure to crucial matches. His clutch plays in the VCT 2023 finals helped FNATIC secure their second title. Chronicle is known for his fast reflexes and ability to adapt to different in-game situations.

Achievements

Chronicle has helped FNATIC secure two VCT Champions titles. His consistency and impact on both offense and defense have solidified him as a core part of the team’s success.
