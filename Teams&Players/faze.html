About

An international esports organization known for its star-studded roster and consistent performances across multiple titles. FaZe Clan has a reputation for being one of the most competitive and resilient teams in the CS2 scene.

History

Founded in 2010, FaZe Clan has established itself as a dominant force in CS2 with a legacy of championships and world-class talent. The team continues to push boundaries with a mix of experience and raw skill, making them one of the most respected organizations in esports.
